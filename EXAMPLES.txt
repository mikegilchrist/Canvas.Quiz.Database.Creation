Example (offline/mock -> canonical JSON -> HTML + SQLite)

1) Put your mock payloads in ./data/mock_api/
   - mock_quiz_submissions.json
   - mock_quiz_questions.json
   - mock_quiz_submission_<id>_questions.json

2) Convert mocks to canonical JSON:
   python3 src/canvas.api.mock_to_json.py data/mock_api --outdir output/JSON --verbose

3) Render HTML:
   python3 src/canvas.api.json_to_html.py output/JSON --outdir output/HTML --verbose

4) Load SQLite:
   python3 src/canvas.api.json_to_sqlite.py output/JSON output/SQLITE/quiz_archive.sqlite --verbose

5) Or do 3+4 together:
   python3 src/canvas.api.json_to_all.py output/JSON output/SQLITE/quiz_archive.sqlite --html-outdir output/HTML --verbose
